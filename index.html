Driven by a passion for understanding and improving human health, I am broadly interested in leveraging existing sensory and biomedical data to enhance well-being, performance, and overall quality of life. I'm currently working at the intersection of imaging and genetics to better understand Autism — specifically, developing methods to address the challenge of missing modalities in multimodal data.
